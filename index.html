About the studio

A different kind
of beauty space.

Ivory Grove is built around the belief that beauty appointments should feel good — not rushed. Our studio is white, light, and deliberately calm. No loud music, no waiting area chaos.

Every treatment is fully personalised. We take time to understand your skin, your goals, and your preferences before we begin. The result is work that looks refined — not overdone.

We see fewer clients than a typical salon. That's intentional. Longer appointments, more considered results, and a level of attention that shows in the finish.

Natural results — no heavy-handed finishes
Extended appointment times as standard
Professional-grade products only
Fully qualified, insured therapists
The people behind the studio

Meet the Team

Founder & Senior Beauty Therapist

Amelia Hart

Amelia founded Ivory Grove after twelve years working in some of Essex's most respected beauty clinics. She specialises in skin treatments and brow design, with a reputation for results that look effortlessly natural. She trains each member of the team personally.

Lash & Brow Specialist

Sophie Lane

Sophie's precision with lash work has made her one of the most requested therapists in the studio. Trained in LVL, Nouveau Lashes, and HD Brows, she approaches every client with a fresh eye and a careful hand. Her work is subtle, intentional, and long-lasting.

Beauty Therapist

Ella Brooks

Ella covers a wide range of treatments — from waxing and nails to prescriptive facials — bringing warmth and precision to every appointment. Recently trained in advanced peel treatments, she is steadily building a loyal following for her thorough and considered approach.
